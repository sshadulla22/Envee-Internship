Our Story

Envee Wheels is a pioneering Indian enterprise in the electric scooter industry, positioned to thrive amidst the growing shift towards sustainable transportation.

With a strong emphasis on innovation, quality, and design, the company's in-house R&D and manufacturing facility in Maharashtra has propelled them to create the first government-approved 72V electric scooter in India's non RTO registered category.

Their mission to contribute to a greener environment and revolutionize the electric vehicle market is backed by collaborations with renowned research scientists and a vision to be listed as a Public Limited company by 2025.

Envee Wheels' strategic focus on creating advanced, eco-friendly electric vehicles marks them as a player in the green mobility revolution. Their commitment to innovation and environmental consciousness, demonstrated by their pioneering 72V electric scooter and collaborations with research experts, positions them well for success as they aspire to list as a Public Limited company and shape the future of electric transportation in India and beyond.
